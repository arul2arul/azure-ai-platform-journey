into English/Tamil for personal understanding.

2. Translation: Azure AI Translator (Hindi text → English/Tamil text)
3. (Optional v2) Text-to-speech for dubbed audio output

## Maps to exam prep

- Text Analysis/Translator module → step 2

## IMPORTANT — usage constraint
Personal use only (own understanding). Do NOT download/republish translated or
dubbed versions of someone else's YouTube content — copyright/ToS risk.
Check first whether YouTube's built-in auto-translate/auto-dub already covers
this channel before building anything.
